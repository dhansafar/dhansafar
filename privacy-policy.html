Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

DhanSafar (“we,” “our,” or “us”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application (“App”) or services.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide: Account information (Name, email, phone), financial data (budgets, expenses), and subscription details.

b. Automatically Collected: Device model, operating system, IP address, and app usage logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to provide app features, process payments securely, communicate updates, and improve overall app functionality through usage analysis.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Sharing only occurs with trusted service providers (payment processors), for legal compliance (court orders), or during business transfers (mergers).

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard 256-bit encryption to safeguard your data. While we strive for absolute security, no method of digital storage is 100% impenetrable.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal information at any time via the app settings or by contacting our support team.

If you have questions about this policy, please reach out to us at contact.dhansafar@gmail.com.
